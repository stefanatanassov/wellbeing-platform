Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how personal data is collected, used, and protected when using this platform and related services.

1. Scope of the Platform

The platform is designed for use by professional trainers and coaches to support training analysis and planning. Athletes participate only by invitation from a trainer and must explicitly authorize access to their data.

The platform provides informational summaries and insights to support professional training decisions. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or automated coaching.

2. Data Collection

Personal data is collected only after explicit user authorization through Garminâ€™s official authorization mechanisms (Garmin Connect APIs).

Collected data may include summary-level historical information such as:

  • Activity summaries (type, duration, intensity indicators)
  • Training load or workload proxies
  • Sleep duration and high-level sleep quality indicators
  • Rest versus training day patterns

The platform intentionally does not collect raw GPS routes, second-by-second heart rate data, or real-time monitoring data.

3. Purpose of Data Use

Data is used solely to provide trainers with longitudinal insights, trend analysis, and AI-assisted summaries to support training planning and evaluation.

Personal data is not used for advertising and is not sold to third parties.

4. Use of AI

AI features are used exclusively to summarize historical patterns, highlight trends, and surface considerations for trainer review. AI does not prescribe workouts, override trainer decisions, or provide health or medical advice.

5. Data Access & Sharing

Trainer access to athlete data is permitted only after explicit athlete consent. Athletes may revoke access at any time.

Personal data is not shared with third parties, except where required to operate core platform functionality or to comply with legal obligations.

6. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored securely using reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. Access is restricted to systems necessary for platform operation.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide platform functionality. Athletes may request deletion of their data at any time.

8. User Rights

Users have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal data. Requests can be made using the contact information below.
