Integration Intent & Scope

Last updated: 1 February 2026

Overview

This platform is a trainer-first decision-support system designed to help professional coaches scale personalized training using Garmin data.

The platform provides longitudinal visibility into athlete training and recovery trends, supported by AI-assisted summaries, while all training execution remains on Garmin devices and within Garmin Connect.

The platform does not replace trainers, provide medical advice, or automate coaching decisions. It is intended to support professional judgment, not override it.

Primary Users

  • Primary users: Professional trainers and coaches
  • Secondary participants: Athletes, by trainer invitation only
  • Athletes must explicitly authorize access to their Garmin data
  • Athletes are not charged directly by the platform

Data Access & Scope

Accessed (summary-level, historical data only):

  • Activity summaries (type, duration, intensity indicators)
  • Training load or equivalent workload proxies
  • Sleep duration and high-level sleep quality indicators
  • Rest versus training day patterns

Explicitly excluded (initial version):

  • Raw GPS routes
  • Second-by-second heart rate data
  • Real-time monitoring or alerts

This scope is intentionally limited to support trend and pattern analysis, not live intervention.

Use of AI

AI is used strictly for trainer decision support. Specifically, AI is used to:

  • Summarize trends across weeks and months
  • Highlight correlations between training load, recovery, and consistency
  • Flag potential overreaching or under-recovery patterns
  • Surface considerations for trainer review

AI does not:

  • Prescribe workouts
  • Override trainer decisions
  • Provide health or medical advice
  • Act as an autonomous coach

All training decisions remain the responsibility of the trainer.

Training Execution

Training plans are created and adjusted by trainers. Training execution occurs entirely on Garmin devices and within Garmin Connect.

The platform does not attempt to replace Garmin’s training creation or execution experience. It supports trainers in managing and evaluating training delivered through Garmin’s ecosystem.

Consent, Privacy & Data Handling

  • Athlete data is accessed only after explicit user authorization via Garmin’s official mechanisms
  • Trainers cannot access athlete data without athlete consent
  • Athletes may revoke access or request data deletion at any time
  • Only summary-level data required for platform functionality is retained
  • Personal data is not sold or shared with third parties

Medical & Regulatory Position

The platform is non-medical and non-diagnostic. It provides informational summaries to support professional training decisions and is not positioned as a healthcare product.

Monetization

The platform is monetized via trainer subscriptions. Trainers bundle the cost into their own services. Athletes are not charged directly by the platform.

Garmin Alignment & Long-Term Vision

Garmin devices are required to ensure data consistency and training execution. The platform is Garmin-first by design.

Longer-term goals include supporting professional adoption of Garmin devices and helping trainers recommend appropriate Garmin products based on athlete needs.
